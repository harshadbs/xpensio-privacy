Privacy Policy for Xpensio

Last updated: March 26, 2026

Xpensio is a fully offline personal finance app. We do not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal data.

Data Storage

All data you enter (transactions, budgets, goals, reminders) is stored locally on your device only. It never leaves your device.

Permissions We Use

  • Camera: Used only to scan receipts locally. Photos are never stored or transmitted.
  • SMS: Read locally to detect bank transaction messages and suggest entries. SMS content is never transmitted or stored outside the app.
  • Notifications: Used only for local reminders you set yourself.

Third-Party Services

The optional bill scanner sends receipt images to OCR.space for text extraction only. No personal or financial data is sent — only the receipt image.
OCR.space privacy policy: https://ocr.space/privacypolicy

Children's Privacy

Xpensio does not collect data from anyone, including children under 13.
